Project Site Plan

Site Name

The chosen name is Book Connoisseurs Club, which reflects a community of book lovers sharing their passion.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a platform for book enthusiasts, providing information on book recommendations, reviews, and a membership form for joining the club.

Scenarios

  • What are the best books to read for personal development?
  • How can I join the Book Connoisseurs Club?

Color Schema

Selected colors for the site:

  • #2C3E50 - Used for headings and accents.
  • #003366 - Used for background colors.

Typography

Selected fonts for the site:

  • Montserrat - Used for body text.
  • Poppins - Used for headings.
